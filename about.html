HISTORY


Pizza Goes Global: With the influx of Italian immigrants in the late 19th and early 20th centuries,
pizza started gaining popularity beyond Naples and Italy. It made its way to the United States with the wave
of Italian immigrants in the late 1800s. The first pizzeria in the United States, Lombardi's, opened in New York City in 1905.
